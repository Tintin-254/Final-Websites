FUNGUO KENYA

Youth Leading for a Better Future

Fostering Unlimited Networks, Growth, and Outstanding Youth (FUNGUO) is a youth-led community-based organization in Migori County, Kenya, dedicated to empowering young people through transformative programs in:

What we do:

Development

Ending Triple Threats - Combating HIV, early pregnancies, and gender-based violence among youth. Youth Empowerment - Skills development, mentorship, and leadership training. Climate Change Action - Environmental conservation, sustainable agriculture, and green solutions. Agri-Business Development - Enhancing food security and economic opportunities for youth. Governance & Civic Education - Promoting informed participation in governance and policy-making. Gender Equity & Boychild Empowerment - Advocating for balanced opportunities for all genders.
